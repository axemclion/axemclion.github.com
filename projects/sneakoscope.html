Sneak-O-Scope
Schedule tweets to be send to your twitter account from a file.
Source Code http://code.google.com/p/sneakoscope/
Project News http://dy-verse.blogspot.com/search/label/sneakoscope
Opensocial Gadget URL http://www.orkut.co.in/Main#Application?appId=425100354110
Sneak-O-Scope is an application that lets you track visitors to your social network profiles. Written in Opensocial, the application can be embedded in Orkut and MySpace for now.
It is visible on the profile page and records details like Visitor Name, IP Address, time spent on your profile, etc of the visitor.
Just ensure that you move the application such that it is expanded on your profile page.
